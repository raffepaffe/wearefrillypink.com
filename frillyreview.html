And here's the review in English, for all you foreign people:

Per-Henrik Petterson and Tomas Amneskog (rather famous Swedish synth-band Kiethevez) took an afternoon off to listen to Frilly Pink.
Heavy dancemusic is the name of the game.
P-HP: Quite heavy, really, but the name's all wrong. Sounds like a glamrock-band.
TA: I think this kind of instrumental music needs more life to be really good. After a minute it feels like you've heard it all.
P-HP: I would definitely have kept on listening after this song. I think it would work on an alternative dancefloor. I give it a 3.
TA: The breaks were good, but there was no action. A little more imagination and it would have been OK, but now it gets a 2.
P-HP: OK, a 2+.
