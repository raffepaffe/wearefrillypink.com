At last! Frilly Pink release their 8th album, called "Welcome to Slavery". It's a collection of B-sides and previously unreleased material from the long career of the band. For those of you who have had no previous contact with the band this is a great opportunity to discover one of the most evil acts of the underground scene. For the die-hard fans there's also a lot of new exciting material never before heard outside the studio.

For example:

Dave Helmet's first ever solo on "Roadkill".

MC Evil breaks it down on "Loops of Death" five years before Drum 'n Bass was even invented.

CC McNasty showing his sensitive side in the ballad "Burn, burn, burn!"

Steve Metal does his famous impersonation of a chainsaw on rock in "Concrete Conquest". This is the only recording that gives you the experience in full surround stereo.

Ben McPetting shows off his tatoos in the hot new video for "Concrete Conquest" which is in the multimedia section on the CD.
